Discover

our products

We offer a range of products , like : Crop Recommendation Model - A model that recommends the best crop to grow in your farm based on the rainfall, humdity, temperature and the soil content. Fertilizer Recommendation Model - A model that recommends the best kind of fertilizer to use on your land based on the crop to be grown and nutrients the soil is lacking. Crop Health Prediction Model - A model that forecasts the health of crops based on the soil content, pesticide used and insect attacks(if any).
